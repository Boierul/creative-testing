i'm very good at it, at getting caught in the thrashing of code. charging at a brick wall again and again until i can't do it any more, until i knock myself out or break the wall or do something terrible.

i don't think i've ever been able to do anything else. the only thing i learned when i was a kid was how to keep going even when you shouldn't, and i have never unlearned that lesson, not even now. i learned to forget and i unlearned remembering.
